Liquid Propellant Rocket Engine Test Stand

2019 - 2020

After having launched Cypress, I started leading an effort to develop a suborbital, spacebound rocket powered by a liquid-propellant rocket engine.

I managed the technical aspects and in the process designed and fabricated large portions of the test stand. The test stand is portable, capable of supporting rocket engines producing in excess of 25kN of thrust and has successfully supported three hot fire tests to date. It remains operational and in use today.
